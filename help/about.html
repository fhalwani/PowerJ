Welcome to the PowerJ Dashboard and Report Generator for Powerpath

PowerJ is a Dashboard application to help management and end-users in insuring smooth workflow and processing of cases and reports in a timely fashion. It aims to help pathology management to make informed decisions on specimen allocation, bottlenecks and case tracking. It provides fine-grained, real-time information about individual cases and specimens being processed.
