The Pie Chart

This chart occupies the top right corner of the main screen to provide a quick breakdown of pending cases. The legend of the chart also displays the actual number of cases in each slide.

Chart Filtering:

You can restrict the chart contents to display only a specific subset of cases by using the 3 drop-down lists in the menubar (2 in the Grossing module). See the "Menu Bar Help Page" for further details.
