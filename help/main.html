Welcome to the PowerJ Dashboard and Report Generator for Powerpath

The main screen includes the following components:

  • A table of pending cases

  • A Pie Chart to summarize pending cases distribution

  • A Bar Chart to summarize daily cases flow

  • A Bar Chart to summarize daily staff workload

  • A Gauge to display the average case turnaround time

  • A Histology Screen to summarize the blocks and slides workload and backlog

  • A Menu Bar to filter case

  • A Staus Bar to scheduled updates and system messages
